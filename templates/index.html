What It Does

Everything You Need to Land Your Dream Job

Smart Job Search

Enter any keyword and job type. The app searches real job boards and returns live listings automatically.

Email Extraction

Automatically scans job descriptions and extracts recruiter and HR email addresses using smart regex.

Auto Email Sending

Sends professional, personalized application emails with your resume via Gmail SMTP.

Resume Upload

Upload your PDF or DOCX resume once. It gets attached to every application email automatically.

Secure & Private

Your credentials are used only for the current session and never stored on any server.

Open Source Python

Built with Flask, beginner-friendly Python code. Easy to understand, modify, and deploy.
